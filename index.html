🔗

Multi-Benefit UX / Single Eligibility API

Platform · Teladoc Health

▼
Problem

Members had to use multiple apps and create multiple accounts to access products from the same company. Eligibility implementation patterns varied across product groups, making it difficult to maintain, troubleshoot, and enhance.

What I Built

Converged multiple eligibility stacks into a single service, offering members their best possible benefit experience across chronic care, primary, mental health, and urgent care — all from one account.

Outcome: Monthly usage in the tens of thousands. Measurable increase in members accessing multiple products. High satisfaction at member and partner levels. Became the foundational eligibility layer for the entire platform.
Platform-wide adoption Tens of thousands of MAU Reduced integration complexity
📋

Claims & Revenue Cycle Pipeline

Platform · Teladoc/Livongo

▼
Problem

A growing pipeline of claims submissions was mostly manual and not fully automated. Variations in payor rules and pricing models made the adjudication process opaque — even to many internal employees, let alone patients.

What I Built

Developed a configurable claims submission pipeline (via CRM) to 30+ payors. Built the standards, processes, and tooling to ensure quality of claims data and patient data across all carrier formats.

Outcome: ~40% of Livongo's platform revenue ran through this pipeline. Reinforced to me why patients find medical bills confusing — adjudication is a true black box, and conversational AI is the right tool to finally explain it in plain language.
~40% of platform revenue 30+ payor integrations
🧠

User Journey · Teladoc Health

Financial Guidance & Cost Transparency

User Journey · Included Health

▼
What I Built

Calculated estimated cost on procedures within the provider search interface. Used a large claims dataset from a self-insurer to build models that surface cost estimates to drive member engagement and care decisions.

Outcome & Takeaway: Used by 1M+ members. 3x increase in user satisfaction and product retention. Financial transparency — making cost predictable — is a dramatically better member experience. Members who understand what they'll pay are more likely to seek care.
1M+ members 3x retention improvement
